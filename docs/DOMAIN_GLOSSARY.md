# Domain Glossary

## Workshop

The automotive repair business using the platform.

---

## Customer

A private individual who owns a vehicle.

---

## Company

A business customer requiring work approval workflows.

---

## Vehicle

A customer-owned asset serviced by the workshop.

---

## Work Order

A repair or maintenance request.

Lifecycle:

- Received
- Diagnosis
- Approval
- Repair
- Delivery

---

## Quote

A budget proposal associated with a Work Order.

---

## Approval

Digital authorization for a Quote.

---

## Part

A replacement component used in repairs.

---

## Supplier

A company that provides parts.

---

## Attachment

A file associated with a vehicle, work order, or supplier purchase.

Examples:

- Photos
- PDFs
- Invoices

---

## Vehicle History

The complete maintenance timeline of a vehicle.

---

## Public Vehicle History

Public subset of vehicle history exposed through QR codes.

---

## AI Assistant

Internal workshop assistant powered by OpenAI.

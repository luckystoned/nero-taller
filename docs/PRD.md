# Nero Automotive CRM

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

### Executive Summary

Nero Automotive CRM is an intelligent workshop management platform designed for automotive repair shops.

The platform centralizes:

- Daily operations
- Vehicle history
- Work orders
- Quotes
- Suppliers
- Parts
- Customer communication

Unlike traditional workshop CRMs, Nero includes an AI Assistant capable of:

- Querying workshop data using natural language
- Providing mechanical assistance
- Suggesting diagnostics
- Supporting workshop operations

The platform combines:

- Lightweight ERP
- Customer CRM
- Vehicle CRM
- Automotive medical record
- QR traceability system
- AI operational assistant

---

## Problem Statement

Most automotive workshops still rely on:

- Paper forms
- WhatsApp conversations
- Phone calls
- Manual tracking

This creates:

- Loss of historical information
- Poor traceability
- Difficult reporting
- Limited operational visibility
- Dependence on mechanic memory

---

## Product Goals

### Operational Goals

Allow users to:

- Register customers
- Register vehicles
- Create work orders
- Manage quotes
- Manage approvals
- Register suppliers
- Register parts
- Track costs
- Maintain vehicle history

### Business Goals

Improve:

- Traceability
- Follow-up times
- Customer communication
- Cost control
- Profitability analysis
- Customer retention

### AI Goals

Allow:

- Natural language operational queries
- Historical queries
- Mechanical assistance
- Diagnostic suggestions
- Future voice-based data entry

## MVP Scope

### Included

- Customer management
- Company management
- Vehicle management
- Work orders
- Quotes
- Approvals
- Parts
- Suppliers
- Attachments
- Dashboard
- Internal AI assistant
- WhatsApp notifications
- Public QR vehicle history

### Excluded

- Real-time stock management
- Online payments
- Government invoicing
- Native mobile app
- Full multi-tenant support
- Advanced OCR
- Advanced scheduling

---

## User Types

### Workshop Owner

Responsible for:

- Operations
- Metrics
- Costs
- Suppliers
- Customers

### Mechanic

Responsible for:

- Diagnostics
- Work orders
- Repairs
- Vehicle history
- AI usage

### Company Customer

Can:

- Approve quotes
- Review pending requests

Access only through approval links.

### Individual Customer

Can only access vehicle public history through QR.

---

## Success Criteria

The MVP is successful when the workshop can:

1. Manage all work orders digitally.
2. Access complete vehicle history.
3. Approve company jobs digitally.
4. Send automatic completion notifications.
5. Query data through AI.
6. Maintain public QR history.
7. Access operational metrics from the dashboard.
